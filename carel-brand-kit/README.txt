USAGE NOTES
  • Color ratio ≈ paper 70 / ink 20 / indigo 7 / brick 3. Brick earns its moments.
  • Keep brick for large text, tags and accents — not body copy.
  • Clear space around the logo = height of the "C". Don't stretch, recolor, or refont it.
  • Frame photos on paper with a #E0DACD hairline so images stay the hero.
