The mark

One handwritten signature carries the whole brand. It stays as drawn — never re-typed, re-spaced, or set in a font. Everything else stays quiet so the mark, and the photographs, lead.

Compact mark

Your C flows into the word, so the small-space mark frames the full wordmark rather than isolating a letter. Use the badge for app icons and the avatar for profiles.

002

Color

Warm paper does most of the work. Ink for text. Indigo is the primary accent — buttons, links, structure. Brick is the spark: tags, a hover, a single highlight. Used sparingly, it stays special.

Contrast: ink on paper is fully legible for body text. Indigo on paper is strong for links and buttons. Keep brick for large text, tags, and UI accents — not body copy. For photos, frame them on paper with a hairline #E0DACD so images stay the hero.

For the build
